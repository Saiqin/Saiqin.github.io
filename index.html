About me

Saiqin Xu received the B.S. degree in the Computer Science and Technology, Anhui Normal University, China, in 2019. I am currently working toward the Ph.D. degree with National Laboratory of Radar Signal Processing, Xidian University, Xi’an, China. My supervisor is Prof. Baixiao Chen. Since October 2022, I have been a Visiting Student with SPRITZ Security and Privacy Research Group, Department of Mathematics, University of Padova, Italy, with the support of China Scholarship Council. Working with Prof. Mauro Conti and PhD. Alessandro Brighente. My research interests include signal processing, parameter estimation, radar systems engineering, artificial neural networks and security and privacy for wireless vehicular communications.
